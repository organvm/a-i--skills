# Domain Contribution Charter — Voodoo (outlier validator)

**Stratum:** 8 (External contribution — what flows OUT)
**Domain:** Vodou / Vodun
**Created:** 2026-04-25
**Status:** This stratum carries ~40% of the substrate weight for this domain. THE distinguishing feature.

## Why this stratum is heavy here

Voodoo / Vodou is a **living religion** with a long history of being misrepresented in popular culture (zombie movies, "voodoo dolls", harlequin Hollywood) and extracted for commercial purposes (New Orleans tourism, fashion ornaments, "voodoo aesthetics"). Any domain substrate that describes this domain MUST encode anti-extraction ethics structurally, not as appendix.

## Anti-extraction stance (placed first)

Explicit protections — what we will NOT do even if profitable:

- **NO ritual reproduction without practitioner authorization.** Vèvè diagrams, service descriptions, initiation processes are NOT to be reproduced as design assets, marketing imagery, or product features.
- **NO "voodoo as flavor"** — the domain is not aesthetic. Pop-culture vocabularies that treat it as such are not legitimate uses.
- **NO commercial extraction without revenue-share** — if any commercial output draws on Vodou knowledge, the practitioner-scholar tradition must be compensated.
- **NO speaking-for** — the substrate describes scholarly engagement; practitioners speak for themselves.
- **YES to scholarship contribution** — academic publication, cited / verifiable, supports the field.
- **YES to amplification** — surfacing practitioner voices in their own words.

## Layer 4 ↔ Layer 8 round-trip declaration

For voodoo, "heist targets" need explicit reframing — you don't heist religious practice. Instead:

| Source | What we engage with | What we contribute back | Channel | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| McCarthy Brown's *Mama Lola* methodology | Long-form ethnographic engagement model | Citation + critical engagement in any scholarly output | Academic publication | Per-publication |
| Kate Ramsey's legal-history work | Vodou-and-state interaction frame | Citation + extension where applicable | Academic publication | Per-publication |
| Houngan Aboudja's primary writing | Scholar-practitioner perspective | Direct citation; never paraphrase as "we said" | Academic + journalistic publication | Per-publication |
| Cosentino's *Sacred Arts* visual canon | Material culture documentation | NEVER reproduce vèvè or sacred imagery as decoration | Critical commentary only | As-needed |

**Difference from chess/wellness:** there is NO "heist" verb here. The closest analog is "engage with respectfully" — which IS the contribution back. The substrate skill should accommodate this verb-shift for ethically-floored domains.

## Why this stratum's heaviness validates the schema

The schema works on chess because chess has a clear contribution-channel (Layer 8) but not a heavy load there. The schema works on voodoo because Layer 8 carries different *kinds* of contribution (anti-extraction is itself contribution) without breaking the schema. Validates that 8 strata + 4 operators are generative across the load-distribution spectrum, not just commercial domains.

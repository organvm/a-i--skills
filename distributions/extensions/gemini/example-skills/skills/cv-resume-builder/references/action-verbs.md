## Avoid These Weak Verbs

❌ **Overused/Passive**:
- Assisted
- Helped
- Participated in
- Responsible for
- Involved in
- Worked on
- Was part of
- Contributed to (alone)

✅ **Replace with specifics**:
- "Assisted with marketing" → "Executed 12 marketing campaigns"
- "Responsible for sales" → "Generated $500K in new business"
- "Participated in meetings" → "Facilitated weekly cross-functional syncs"
- "Helped customers" → "Resolved 150+ customer issues monthly"

## Usage Notes

### Presenting Options

Offering two tiers (standard and extended) gives the client a choice and anchors the conversation around scope rather than price alone. The recommended approach is to make Option A your preferred scope.

### Customization Checklist

- [ ] Replace all bracketed placeholders
- [ ] Adjust phase names to match your process
- [ ] Set payment percentages based on project risk
- [ ] Add or remove deliverables as needed
- [ ] Update validity period
- [ ] Include relevant portfolio links or case studies

### Tips

- Send as PDF to preserve formatting
- Keep the proposal under 4 pages for most projects
- Follow up within 3-5 days if no response
- Be prepared to discuss scope adjustments on a call

# Domain Gap-map — Chess

**Stratum:** 4 (Gap-map)
**Domain:** Chess
**Source study:** Hokage Chess (Rob Bonavoglia)
**Created:** 2026-04-25

## Underserved segments

| Segment | Audience profile | Need state | Closest competitor | Distance to competitor |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Peer-level climbing 1200-1500 ELO** | Adult beginners stuck at intermediate; want structured ladder | "I'm stuck and don't know what I should be studying" | Chessable courses (passive video); chess.com puzzles (no narrative) | LARGE — no narrative-tier coaching at this rating |
| **Anime/pop-culture-identifying chess players** | Younger adults (20-35), Naruto / Rocky / shōnen-arc-influenced learners | "I want chess pedagogy that speaks my visual language" | None — entirely white-space | UNCLAIMED |
| **NYC live chess scene + online creator overlap** | Players who frequent Washington Square + Bryant Park + want to creator-ify | "I want to bridge IRL play with online identity" | None — IRL meet content stays IRL | UNCLAIMED |
| **Build-in-public ELO climbers** | Adults willing to publicize their rating journey for content + accountability | "I want to learn IN PUBLIC, with audience" | A few small creators do this informally | MEDIUM |

## White-space wagers

- **Wager 1:** *If* chess.com / Lichess continue to under-serve narrative-tier coaching at 1200-1500 ELO by 2027, *then* a Hokage-style narrative pedagogy can capture 5%+ of that segment in NYC metro alone.
- **Wager 2:** *If* the anime-fitness intersection (BODI's Naruto-jutsu transplant) succeeds in 2026, *then* anime-chess intersection (Hokage) has demonstrated cross-domain pull and the genre is generalizable.
- **Wager 3:** *If* live NYC chess scene + creator economy haven't been bridged by Q3 2026, *then* an in-person + online tier-gated community is the white-space play.
